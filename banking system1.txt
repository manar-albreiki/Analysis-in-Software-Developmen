| Acceptance Criteria Sample |
------------------------------
- Create Account: User can register with valid info; email verified; confirmation sent.
- Login: Username/password required; OTP enabled; error shown for invalid credentials.
- Transfer Money: Destination valid; OTP confirmation; balances updated.
- Download Statement: PDF generated with accurate transactions.
- Freeze Account: Admin can freeze account; transactions blocked; audit logged.
